Lasagna

Classic lasagna recipe - you can't go wrong with this one!

Ingredients needed

  • Lasagna noodles: You can use either precooked or ones you have to boil.
  • 3/4 pound of ground beef.
  • Half a cup of minced onion
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic - crusheds
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • A pound of mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 egg
